Carbon Credit

Carbon credits are a market-based mechanism designed to reduce global greenhouse gas emissions. One carbon credit represents one ton of CO2 that has been prevented from entering the atmosphere. Companies and individuals can purchase these credits to offset their own carbon emissions.

Benefits of Carbon Credits

Reduces Emissions

Helps lower greenhouse gas emissions by offsetting carbon output.

Sustainable Development

Supports projects that contribute to sustainable development goals.

Regulatory Compliance

Aids in meeting regulatory requirements for carbon reduction.

Economic Growth

Encourages investment in green technologies and sustainable practices.
